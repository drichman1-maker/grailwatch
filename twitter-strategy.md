## Engagement Tactics
- Reply to Apple tweets with price context
- Quote tweet expensive posts with deals
- Ask questions: "What's your price target for [product]?"
- Run polls: "Wait for M4 or buy M3 at discount?"

## Tools Needed
- Scheduling: TweetDeck (free) or Hypefury (paid)
- Analytics: Twitter Analytics (free)
- Automation: n8n for price alerts → tweets
