Pokémon Card Investing 101

Published: February 2026 | Reading time: 10 min

Getting Started

The Pokémon card market has matured. Focus on graded cards (PSA 9-10) of iconic characters from Base Set, Jungle, and Neo series.

Cards to Watch:
  • Base Set Charizard (PSA 9: $8,000-12,000)
  • 1st Edition Lugia Neo Genesis
  • Shadowless Blastoise
  • Modern alt arts (low pop)

Key Rules

  • Buy the card, not the grade (check for qualifiers)
  • Population matters (lower = rarer)
  • Condition is everything
  • Hold for 3-5 years minimum
